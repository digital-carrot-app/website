---
title: Garmin
---

Fitness data from Garmin devices can be shared with Digital Carrot by installing the Garmin Connect app
and configuring it to share data with Apple Health or Google Health Connect.

## iPhone

[Share data with Apple Health](https://support.garmin.com/en-US/?faq=lK5FPB9iPF5PXFkIpFlFPA)

## Android

[Share Data with Google Health Connect](https://support.garmin.com/en-US/?faq=JToBEy0jfe6pIygark2Ui5)
