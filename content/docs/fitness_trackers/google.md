---
title: Google
---

Fitness data from the Google Fit app and devices such as the Pixel Watch can be shared with
Digital Carrot by configuring Fit to share data with Google Health Connect.

## iPhone

Google Fit cannot share health data with Apple Health at the moment.

## Android

[Share Data with Google Health Connect](https://support.google.com/fit/answer/12830119?hl=en)
