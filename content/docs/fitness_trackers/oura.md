Fitness data from Oura Rings can be shared with Digital Carrot by installing the Oura app
and configuring it to share data with Apple Health or Google Health Connect.

## iPhone

[Share data with Apple Health](https://support.ouraring.com/hc/en-us/articles/360025438734-Apple-Health-Integration)

## Android

[Share Data with Google Health Connect](https://support.ouraring.com/hc/en-us/articles/10786105824531-Health-Connect-by-Android-Integration)
