title: Whoop
---
Fitness data from Whoop devices can be shared with Digital Carrot by installing the Whoop app
and configuring it to share data with Apple Health or Google Health Connect.

## iPhone

[Share data with Apple Health](https://support.whoop.com/s/article/Apple-Health-Integration?language=en_US)

## Android

[Share Data with Google Health Connect](https://support.whoop.com/s/article/Google-Health-Integration-For-Android?language=en_US)
